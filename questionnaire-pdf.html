Pre-Session Reflection

Before our session, please answer the following questions as truthfully and thoroughly as you can.

Do not filter. Do not perform. Do not answer the way you think you "should."

This is a space for radical honesty.

The more transparent and specific you are, the faster we can scratch beneath the surface of the problem and identify the real root — not just the symptom. Depth creates transformation. Precision creates results.

Take your time.

Be as specific as possible about the situation you want to work on.

What is happening? What feels stuck or challenging?

For example: fear, frustration, guilt, sadness, anger, confusion, pressure, etc.

What outcome would feel meaningful, relieving, or empowering for you?
