How to Use This Journal

Great decision-making is a skill, not a talent. Like any skill, it improves with deliberate practice and honest reflection. This journal is designed to help you:

  • Document important business decisions before you make them
  • Identify patterns in how you think, what you value, and where your blind spots are
  • Review past decisions to learn from both successes and mistakes
  • Build a personal decision-making framework over time

Complete one entry for each significant business decision. Review your journal monthly to identify patterns.

Tip: Print multiple copies of the Decision Entry template (pages 2-3), or recreate it digitally. The key is consistency — journal every significant decision for at least 90 days to see meaningful patterns emerge.
