Step 4: Correlation Assessment

Estimate or calculate the correlation between your 5 largest positions. Use a scale of -1 (perfectly inverse) to +1 (perfectly correlated).

Where to find correlation data: Portfolio Visualizer (free), Morningstar (premium), or your brokerage's research tools.

Interpretation Guide

  • 0.70 to 1.00: Highly correlated — these positions behave as a single bet
  • 0.40 to 0.69: Moderately correlated — some diversification benefit
  • 0.00 to 0.39: Low correlation — good diversification
  • -0.01 to -1.00: Negative correlation — excellent hedge
